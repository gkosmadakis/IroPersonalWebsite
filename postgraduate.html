4.DNA Extraction and sexing by PCR: The purpose of this experiment was to extract DNA from both whole human blood and blood spots on filter paper and to use PCR to determine the sex of the individuals from whom the blood was taken.For PCR amplification two primers are used each of which annealed to only one site in the entire human genome, so the primer pair will amplify a single fragment of the genome. In this 'sexing' PCR for a sample of male DNA expect to generate two different PCR products and for female should generate only X chromosome PCR product.
